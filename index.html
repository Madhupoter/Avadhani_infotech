    Project Overview

    Avadhani Infotech, Is A First Of Its Kind Office Space Designed Around Nature. Located In Kiadb Hitech/defence Aerospace Park Of Bangalore North, The Location Has An Excellent Access To Airport & Bangalore City, While Providing Eco Friendly, Refreshing And Enjoyable Work Environment With The Most Energy Efficient Design.


    Avadhani Infotech Offers Office Spaces Starting From 15000sq.ft To 3,20,000sq.ft Which Is Available For Immediate Rental.


    It Enjoys Excellent Natural Daylight With Exclusive Landscape In And All Around The Campus. Ample Car Parking Space In The Basement With Easy Entry And Exit. Exellent Cafeteria Facility Is Provided At The Landscaped Terrace Level.

    Concept

    The Project Is Designed To Provide Everything That People Need To Work Productively And Yet Live Life To The Full. The Building Has Generous Reception Area Creating An Excellent First Impression To Visitors, And All The Floors Enjoy Excellent Natural Light And High Flexibility For Partitioning. As Per Clients Requirements The Workspace Is Designed Around "Nature" & Boasts Of A Large Internal Courtyard Around Which The Offices Are Placed For Maximum Light. The Materials Use For The Construction Have Low Environmental Impact While The Internal And External Lighting Solutions Provided Are Keeping In Mind The Best Practices By Using And Low Energy Led’s.
